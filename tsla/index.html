The Shared Life Assembly

About Us

The Shared Life Assembly is an Apostolic hub
geared at raising people who express the God Life
in every sphere of society.

TSLA started as a "fellowship" on the 8th of february 2020 under the leadership
of Rev'd. Christopher Okolo and Rev'd.(Mrs) Favour Christopher.

It became a denominational Church on the 26th of february, 2023.

Vision Statement

Raising a people who express The God-Life in every sphere of society.

Mission Statement

1. Teach the principles of God's Word,
2. Practically express the God-Life in our meetings.
3. Reach out to universities, college's of education,
secondary schools to express and introduce the God-Life
to young people.
4. Take the gospel to the streets, that is, engage in street
evangelism
